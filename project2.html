AI 建模軟體學習
AI modeling software learning

我們使用 AI 建模軟體 Tripo 進行學習，Tripo 是一款 AI 3D 建模工具，
只需要輸入文字描述，就能自動生成對應的 3D 模型。它操作簡單、速度快。
在課堂上，親手打造了自己專屬的模型。這個過程既有趣，也充滿挑戰！

一開始我遇到不少困難，比如輸入的指令不夠清楚、生成的模型與自己相差甚遠。
不過，經過討論與不斷嘗試，我慢慢摸索出適合的參數設定，也從中學到了很多。

In class, we got to try out an AI modeling tool called Tripo, and we each created our own personal 3D model. The whole experience was super fun, but also a bit challenging! At first, we ran into some issues—our prompts weren’t very clear, and the model didn’t really look like us at all. But after talking it through and experimenting with different settings and numbers, we slowly figured things out.
It felt great to finally get a result that we were happy with. Overall, it was a really cool way to learn something new and hands-on with AI.
